EBS Upgrade R12.2

Companies are Upgrading E-Business Suite R12/11i, Learn Oracle EBS Upgrade to R12.2 and Demand Higher Salary …

Oracle E-business Suite (EBS) R12.0 & 11i are already out of support and R12.1 support is available till Dec 2021, more and more companies are upgrading to Oracle E-Business Suite R12.2

Version R12.2 E-Business Suite from Oracle comes with loads of new features and technology component advancements like replacing the underlying Technology Stack from 10g AS to WebLogic Server (aka Fusion MiddleWare), introducing new features like Online Patching (ADOP), Edition Based Redefinition, Dual File System and much more.

Companies need trained Oracle App DBAs who understand Oracle E-Business Suite R12.2 upgrade from both version R12.0/12.1 and version 11i

There are not enough Oracle Apps DBAs who could upgrade to 12.2 or knows things required to upgrade like reducing downtime, best practices, and preparing custom code to support online patching.

With hundreds and thousands of customers on 11i and R12.0 rushing to upgrade their existing Oracle Applications to R12.2, the industry is looking for qualified Oracle Apps DBAs who can do this for them with the lowest turnaround time and the with least possible downtime.

That’s where YOU come in…

In short today’s Companies need skilled Apps DBAs who are able to:

  • Comprehensively understand the new R12.2 Architecture
  • Understand the location of various logfiles and its importance
  • Well versed with ADOP Online Patching Cycle
  • Excellent Troubleshooting skills especially on Fusion Middleware
  • Understand the nuances involved in upgrading E-Business Suite to R12.2
  • Understand and manage CDB/PDB model
  • Understand best practices to reduce Upgrade Downtime

And by the end of this class, you’ll possess all of these skills, and so much more…
