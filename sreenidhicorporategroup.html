Sreenidhi Corporate Group

Sreenidhi Consulting was established in the year 1996, as a comprehensive solution provider to recruitment, staffing, and HR needs. Since then, we have been significantly investing in our core philosophy of ‘happy employees’ with visible results in enhanced productivity for our clients.  We have now grown to be one of the foremost search and staffing companies in India.

Datacore Technologies is a 100% subsidiary of Sreenidhi which focuses on IT consulting, product development, and staffing. With a highly skilled pool of over 500 developers, architects, consultants, project managers, and tech support specialists, Datacore specializes in helping clients reach their competitive edge. This commitment to client success has brought Datacore the Best Technology Leadership award.

Sreenidhi is in turn the Indian subsidiary of the staffing giant Outsourcing Inc Japan, a billion-dollar HR and service contracts outsourcing organisation, which has a presence in 17 countries. The company offers complete solutions with respect to manpower requirements in the form of complete project/process/service delivery, one-time placement, or temporary staffing. Outsourcing Inc has a business turnover of about $1 billion.

With more than 400 in-house employees, 70 recruiters declined locations, 600 software engineers, 3000 BPO staff, and 5000+ non-IT professionals on our rolls working at multiple client locations, we are one of the most recognised and sought-after recruitment and staffing consultants in the country.

Our corporate office is in Bangalore, and we have branch offices in Chennai, Hyderabad, Mumbai, Pune, Delhi, and Noida. Sreenidhi also has an office in the USA.

Our service offerings

Today, Sreenidhi’s service offerings are being considered the benchmarks in the HR industry. We believe that great HR strategies are built around the happiness of employees. Only such strategies will bring out the best from people, and are therefore of paramount importance to any organisation that wants to improve its productivity.

We rely on our state-of-the-art technology backed by proven recruiting processes and a wide network to identify and recruit the best possible candidates for our client organisations. This capability, along with a certain sensitivity that we have internalised from our years of experience, helps us efficiently manage talent and thus makes us the partner of choice for all smart organisations.

Sreenidhi provides comprehensive people related solutions in the following areas:

  • Recruitment (contingency search and placements)
  • Temporary staffing
  • Production/service outsourced delivery
  • IT projects
  • HRMS outsourcing
  • HR operations
  • Payroll and statutory processes
  • Campus placement functions

We have several professionals in multiple domains on our payrolls, whom we can easily access. So we bring to you a phenomenal talent pool like no other. We offer our clients highly personalised and customised services in search, staffing and outsourcing.
